Health Day Events Medical Consultations & Awareness sessions

Our team prepares material to cover different Public Health topics, based on discussions with the collaborating NGO.

Participants receive free medical consultations with physicians and dentists, and awareness sessions from public health and other professionals. At the end of each event, every participant receives a hygiene bag of shampooing, soap, tissue paper, toothbrush and toothpaste.

Fundraising Annual events & ongoing activities

Our fundraising events support us in covering the cost of hygiene kits and a small amount goes to covering the expenses of the Health Day Events.

Want to Join? Lots of opportunities!

If you are at least 18 years old, you can become a volunteer and contribute to enhancing access to medical care and public health awareness. Send us an email on 4allcauses@gmail.com and we will get back to you with all the details!
