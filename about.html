Young professionals give back to the community How it all started

Founded in 2015 by Tamara Lotfi & Andrea Darzi, 4allcauses started as an initiative that gathers young professionals and graduate students from different backgrounds to serve one goal: providing the less fortunate with free medical consultations and education on health & hygiene. With the help of friends, colleauges and family, we collaborated with existing non-political non-discriminatory NGOs in launching a series of Health Day Events.

Our team included medical doctors, nurses, pharmacists, public health professionals, environmental health specialists, business graduates, architects, graphic designers, engineers and others.

From 2015 to 2019, we succeeded in organizing 12 Health Day Events in 5 regions in Lebanon, working with 30 volunteers, at 5 NGOs. The events included Medical Examination (physical, oral and visual) and Awareness sessions (Nutrition/Food safety, Hygiene and Psycho-Social Support). Our capacity now grows beyond Health Day Events, stay tuned to learn about the new plans!
